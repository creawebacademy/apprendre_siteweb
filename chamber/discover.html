Rwenzori National Park

Rwenzori Region, Uganda

This park is a protected reserve with great biodiversity, ideal for hiking and bird watching. It is home to majestic mountains, glaciers, lakes, and exceptional flora and fauna.

National Museum of the Democratic Republic of Congo

Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo

An intriguing museum showcasing the history and culture of Congo, with a rich collection of ancient artifacts, sculptures, and traditional art objects spanning centuries.

Inga Falls

Kongo-Central Province, Democratic Republic of Congo

These impressive waterfalls, located in the west of the DRC, are a must-see for nature and adventure lovers, offering a spectacular view and a peaceful environment amidst the tropical rainforest.

United Nations Square

Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo

The United Nations Square is an iconic site in the heart of the city, used for political gatherings, national events, and celebrations. It is surrounded by important government buildings.

Boboto Cultural Center

Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo

A space dedicated to art and culture, where exhibitions, workshops, and performances are held. This center showcases local talent and promotes cultural exchange with the rest of the world.

Liberty Market

Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo

An energetic market where you can purchase local products, souvenirs, and unique handcrafted items. It's the perfect place to discover traditional products, from crafts to local spices.

Laïkoro Park

Mongala Province, Democratic Republic of Congo

A natural park offering relaxation areas, picnic spots, and hiking trails to explore the local wildlife and flora. This park is a perfect place to enjoy a moment of peace and connect with nature.

Notre-Dame Cathedral of Congo

Kinshasa, Democratic Republic of Congo

A magnificent cathedral, a symbol of Christianity in the region, with its imposing architecture and peaceful atmosphere. A must-visit site for spiritual and cultural visitors.
